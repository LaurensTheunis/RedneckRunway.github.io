Redneck Runway

Intro

Welcome to the first log of Redneck Runway. You can find all the information regarding the game here. This will be editited in the future to match where the development is at right now.

The game

Redneck Runway is a fun platformer. You play as a redneck farmer who's goal is to find his sister to take her out. On the way, you will meet obstacles and enemies that need to be defeated.

Art Style

Pricing

Profit

Redneck Runway will be launched when the closed-beta is over. The game will be free for the first 2 weeks(with ads), after that, the game will cost $ 0,89.

A possibility is that we could get sued for copyright reasons, if that is a threat, we can make the game free. Copyright only applies if any profit is made from the game itself, not its ads.
